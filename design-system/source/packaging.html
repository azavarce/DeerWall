Net content · URL

Six elements. Same order. Every SKU.

1
Wordmark, top & centeredSized to roughly 28% of the panel width. Always orange/white. Never reversed to a single color.
2
SKU badge"Ready to use" or "Concentrate" — a black circle with white mono type. Top-right of the visible panel.
3
Hero deerWhitetail buck against the orange-on-ink color block. Same crop on every SKU; never re-shot per size.
4
Tagline"The natural way to keep deer away" — Inter Tight 700, white, four lines max. Right-aligned to the deer.
5
Trust marksQR to deerwall.global on the lower-left, 100% Natural leaf mark adjacent. Same scale across SKUs.
6
Net content + URLBottom right. Mono. Net volume above, deerwall.global URL below in orange.
