5.2

Construction grid

Every icon is drawn inside an 24×24 frame with a 2px live area and 1.5px stroke. Round caps, round joins, no fills.

24 px frame 2 px live area 1.5 px stroke round caps & joins

Drawn the same way, every time.

RULE 01
24 × 24 frameNative grid. Scale to any size in 4px increments.
RULE 02
2px live-area paddingGlyph never touches the frame. Optical balance over math.
RULE 03
1.5px strokeSingle weight across the entire system. No mixed weights inside an icon.
RULE 04
Round caps & joinsSoft terminals match Newsreader's italic terminals. No miter, no bevel.
RULE 05
Outline onlyNo fills. Color is set on stroke only. Use currentColor.

Don't

Four common mistakes. The icons are quiet on purpose.

Don't fillOutline only. Stroke takes the color.
✕
Don't thickenSingle weight across the system.
✕
Don't thin out1.5px is the floor. Anything less disappears.
✕
Don't mix fill + linePick one system per icon.
