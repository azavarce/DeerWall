Presentation deck

→

Six principles that make every decision easier.

P / 01

Orange is the brand.

One signature color, used with conviction. We don't dilute it with a rainbow of accents — we let it carry the equity.

P / 02

The deer is the proof.

Real photographic deer — not illustrations, not silhouettes — show that we know our subject. Treat them with respect, never as villains.

P / 03

Editorial over corporate.

Confident serif, generous whitespace, plain language. Beats clip-art and gradient gloss every time.

P / 04

Show real customers.

If we have a customer photo, use it. If we don't, mark a placeholder — never fake it with stock smiles.

P / 05

Less surface, more system.

The same components everywhere — packaging, deck, web, retail. Each surface compounds the equity of the others.

P / 06

Read it from ten feet.

Every label, ad, and shelf-talker has to communicate at distance. Hierarchy isn't decoration; it's the job.
