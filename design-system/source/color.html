Accessibility · contrast

Every color combination we ship should clear WCAG AA at 4.5:1 for body text and 3:1 for large text and non-text UI. Combinations that fail are listed too — for awareness.

PairCombinationRatioGradeUse for
Ink 800 / Sand 50 14.8 : 1 AAA Body copy, headlines, default page
Sand 50 / Ink 800 14.8 : 1 AAA Reversed hero copy, dark cards
White / Orange 500 3.4 : 1 AA · LARGE Buttons, headlines > 24px, packaging
White / Orange 700 5.9 : 1 AAA Body text on orange surfaces
Orange 700 / Sand 50 5.9 : 1 AAA Brand body copy, accent text
